# Image Assets — CFTN US

All photography should feel cinematic, documentary, and sacred.
Avoid stock photos. Prefer real moments from actual gatherings.

---

## /hero
The strongest, most atmospheric images. Full-screen background use.
Think: candlelit rooms, lifted hands in low light, musicians mid-worship.
Aspect ratio: landscape, 16:9 or wider. Min resolution: 2400×1400px.

## /worship
Worship and prayer moments — singers, musicians, intercession, altar times.
Can be color or redirected to /bw. Used in About and interior sections.

## /community
Relational and group photos — people connecting before/after gatherings,
small circles praying, worship leaders talking. Warm, human, unposed.

## /cities
Gathering and venue photography tied to specific cities.
Skylines, church interiors, stage setups, crowd worship moments.
Named by city where possible: `nashville-01.jpg`, `atlanta-02.jpg`.

## /bw
Black-and-white documentary photography.
High contrast, film-grain-friendly. Used for visual texture and editorial moments.
Converts well from any of the above categories.

## /media
Placeholder folder for future worship recordings, album art, and video thumbnails.
When music or video releases are added, store cover art and stills here.
